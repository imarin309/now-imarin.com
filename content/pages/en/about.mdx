Hi, I'm imarin.
This blog is a place where I write about things I enjoy, mainly anime, games.

I have spent much of my life with anime and games since around my junior high and high school years. I watch many kinds of anime, but I especially like the Gundam series. For games, I usually enjoy RPGs and visual novels when playing alone, and titles like the Gundam Versus series or Super Smash Bros. when playing competitively. Recently, I have also been getting into Gunpla, so I would like to write about that as a record of my progress.

## About the Blog Title

For me, one of the biggest appeals of anime and games is the feeling of immersion that lets you step away from everyday life. When I am absorbed in a fictional world, I can stop thinking about unnecessary worries for a while.

There have been many times when I was caught up in regrets about the past or anxieties about the future. Thoughts like "Why did I make that mistake today?" or "What if I become seriously ill ten years from now?" can be difficult to shake off. In those moments, playing games or building Gunpla has helped me focus on the present. That is why I chose the English title "In the Moment."

## A Short Greeting

My writing may be rough at times, but I hope these posts help readers share the feelings that come from watching anime or playing games. If an article makes your day a little more enjoyable, I would be very happy.
