I just finished watching Classroom of the Elite Season 4 (Year 2, First Semester arc)!

https://you-zitsu.com/

In a word: :large[it was incredibly fun!]

I looked forward to watching Classroom of the Elite every week, catching up on my phone during the commute train.
True to the tagline "Among them lurks a White Room assassin targeting Ayanokoji," every new first-year character seemed to be hiding some kind of ulterior motive, and I was always excited during the ending, imagining what would happen next.

## The Deserted Island Exam: Settled With Fists

In the end, everyone in the deserted island exam settled things through battle — pure violence ( ^ω^ )

- Amasawa vs Kushida
- Nanase vs Ayanokoji
- The first-year squad vs. the Sakayanagi squad
- Housen vs Ryuen
- Ayanokoji vs Nagumo
- Horikita & Ibuki vs Amasawa
- Ayanokoji & senior Kiryuin vs. the acting chairman and his ally teacher

That's just what I could think of off the top of my head.

### Even the Acting Chairman Throws Down

By the final episode, even the acting chairman :large[cornered Ayanokoji on the deserted island and, teaming up with a fellow teacher, ganged up on him two-against-one.]

:red[Whoa, hold on, is that really okay for a teacher to do!?] I couldn't help but laugh.

If he's willing to resort to such extreme measures, I can't help but wonder why he doesn't just use his authority as chairman to expel Ayanokoji outright.
Also, I secretly thought that if someone filmed the beating, it could actually be used to bring down the acting chairman instead.

But honestly, :large[the battle animation was so incredible that none of that mattered — I was completely pulled in, and Ayanokoji was just cool.]

Ayanokoji completely saw through their dirty trick — throwing sand in his eyes while attacking with a hidden stun gun — and overwhelmed them anyway.

There were plenty of great battles this cour alone, but Ayanokoji's fights always feel like they're on a completely different level, which makes them so fun to watch.

## The Charming New First-Years

### Amasawa

Next up, my other favorite moment was :blue[the clash between Amasawa and Kushida.]
Kushida's pettiness was on full display, and Amasawa absolutely wrecked her to put her in her place... :pink[this is exactly what I'd been wanting to see (lol)]

Honestly, Amasawa's presence alone makes this story so much more fun.

To put it plainly, Amasawa turned out to be the White Room assassin the tagline was hyping up, but :red[in reality she's just a fangirl devoted to Ayanokoji.]

And that's what makes her so adorable!

In the final episode she fights Horikita and Ibuki two-on-one and handles it with total ease, radiating strong-character energy — yet she talks like a gyaru and is secretly devoted to the protagonist. How could anyone not fall for that?

### Nanase

My personal heroine of Season 4... has to be Nanase Tsubasa.

For some reason, this beautiful underclassman starts tagging along with Ayanokoji once the deserted island exam begins.
They camp together through the night, and she shows off a scorching swimsuit on the beach — stacking up heroine moves left and right.
(Karuizawa, you're allowed to be more jealous than that!)

It turns out she'd lured him deep into the mountains to challenge him to a fair fight, only to be utterly crushed — after which she follows him around like a loyal dog.

:red[Her cool, strong-character first impression contrasted with her adorable underling behavior later on is just great.]

Amasawa included, the new first-year characters are genuinely fun and interesting.

## I Want Karuizawa to Get Her Happy Ending

Ayanokoji and Karuizawa's relationship as a couple feels completely settled in by now.
Karuizawa gets flustered finding another girl's hair in Ayanokoji's room, and she's clearly on edge watching him spend time with Nanase on the deserted island.

Ayanokoji, for his part, has apparently been thinking ahead about relief points in case Karuizawa gets expelled — which made me a little happy, wondering if he actually cares about her more than expected...

Then near the end, :pink[Ichinose, of all people, confesses her feelings to him.]

Wait, hold on!
I kind of sensed it coming, but is this turning into a full harem pointed straight at Ayanokoji!?
Well, it was pretty obvious just from looking at the ending illustrations ( ・∇・)

Personally, I just want Karuizawa, who devotedly loves him so earnestly, to find happiness...

## Closing Thoughts

I recently changed jobs and started commuting to the office more often, which had me feeling a bit down, but looking forward to Classroom of the Elite every week made riding the train genuinely enjoyable.
Thank you for making every week fun.

Looks like Season 5 has already been confirmed, so I'll be looking forward to it patiently.
